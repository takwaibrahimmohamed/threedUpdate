Why not just hire a full-time developer?
Effective development requires a diverse team, not just one individual. Projects often need varied skills‚Äîfront-end, back-end, UI/UX design, quality assurance, and project management. Hiring a full-time team to cover all these areas can cost anywhere between $30,000 to $60,000 monthly, which is a significant investment. By partnering with our agency, you gain access to a collective pool of expertise, resources, and capabilities at a fraction of the cost, ensuring high-quality, efficient, and flexible solutions for your development needs.
Is there a limit to how many development requests I can submit?
No limits here. Once subscribed, you're welcome to submit unlimited development requests. Our team addresses them sequentially, ensuring each task receives the focused attention it deserves for top-quality outcomes.
How quickly will my development tasks be completed?
We strive to complete individual tasks within a 48-hour timeframe. This quick turnaround ensures your project progresses efficiently, without compromising the high standards of quality we uphold.
Who will be working on my projects?
Projects are managed by our core team, under the direct oversight of our founder. For specialized tasks, we collaborate with expert partners, ensuring every aspect of your project benefits from top-tier expertise.
How does the pause feature work?
Understanding that development needs can vary, we offer the option to pause your subscription. Our billing cycles run on a 31-day basis, allowing unused days to be carried forward and utilized whenever your project requires.
What technologies and platforms do you develop with?
Our team is proficient in a comprehensive array of technologies and platforms, ensuring full-stack development capabilities for any project scope.
How do I submit development requests?
Requests can be submitted through our project management tool, catering to the preferred communication style of our clients.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
Your satisfaction is our top priority. We're committed to revising the work until it aligns perfectly with your vision.
Are there any types of development work you don't do?
We cover a broad spectrum of development tasks, focusing on areas where we can deliver the highest value.
What if I only have one development request?
You can pause your subscription afterwards and reactivate it when new needs arise, ensuring you fully utilize your subscription without any waste.
What about refunds?
Due to the collaborative and high-quality nature of our work, we do not offer refunds. However, we believe in open communication and revisions to ensure your project meets your standards and expectations.
